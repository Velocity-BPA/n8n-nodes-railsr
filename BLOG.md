# Automate Embedded Finance with Our New Railsr Node for n8n

We're excited to announce the launch of our latest community node: n8n-nodes-railsr! At Velocity BPA, we're constantly working to expand the n8n ecosystem with powerful integrations, and this one opens up incredible possibilities for embedded finance automation.

## What Problem Does This Solve?

Building financial products is complex. Between managing ledgers, processing multi-rail payments, and handling KYC/KYB compliance, fintech teams often spend countless hours on manual processes and custom integrations. The Railsr platform provides robust embedded finance infrastructure, but connecting it to your broader workflow automation has been a challenge—until now.

With the n8n-nodes-railsr integration, you can seamlessly incorporate Railsr's powerful financial services into your n8n workflows. Whether you're building a neobank, payment platform, or any financial application, you can now automate ledger operations, payment processing, and compliance checks without writing extensive custom code.

## Key Features

The n8n-nodes-railsr node brings Railsr's core capabilities directly into your automation workflows:

- **Ledger Management**: Automatically create accounts, track balances, and manage transactions across your financial infrastructure
- **Multi-Rail Payments**: Process payments across different payment rails including cards, bank transfers, and more
- **KYC/KYB Automation**: Streamline customer and business verification processes with automated compliance workflows
- **Real-time Updates**: Trigger workflows based on transaction events, status changes, and account activities

Imagine automatically onboarding new customers with KYC checks, creating their ledger accounts, and sending welcome notifications—all in a single visual workflow. Or processing payment callbacks that automatically update your CRM, send receipts, and trigger fulfillment processes.

## Getting Started

Installing the Railsr node is simple. Within your n8n instance, run:


npm install n8n-nodes-railsr


After installation, restart n8n, and you'll find the Railsr node available in your node palette. Configure your Railsr API credentials, and you're ready to start building powerful embedded finance automations.
